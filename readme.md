Bundle up. We're heading into the mountains.

<br>

Alpine Whispers transforms the highlands into a serene yet lively alpine escape. Snow drifts over timber roofs, distant bells echo through valleys, and the wilderness feels vast, cold, and beautifully alive.

<br>

Climb higher. Breathe deeper. Stay warm.

<br>

## Life in the Highlands

<br>

- Alpine Sheep with unique wool variants  
- Reindeer that can be ridden by two players  
- Wildlife adapted to harsh, snowy climates  

<br>

The mountains are not empty. They are simply quiet.

<br>

## Revitalized Winter Biomes

<br>

- Improved Snowy Plains and Grove biomes  
- Richer terrain shaping and layered snow detail  
- Enhanced vegetation and alpine atmosphere  

<br>

Every step crunches beneath your boots.

<br>

## Alpine Craftsmanship

<br>

- New block sets inspired by traditional Central European timber architecture  
- Cozy wood variants and structural details  
- Perfect materials for lodges, chalets, and mountain villages  

<br>

Build where the frost settles.

<br>

## Winter Details

<br>

- Icicles hanging from frozen rooftops  
- Wreaths and festive alpine decor  
- Subtle seasonal touches to complete your snowy builds  

<br>

Small details. Deep immersion.
